My FirstDay In The Bootcamp

I remember being very excited because I had no idea of programming and wanted to learn a lot to have better job offers. It was like chaos ... I had the feeling that my eyes wouldn't stop spinning and I felt like I was going to faint. Too much information that also sounded like Chinese. They threw us into the pool with the phrase "you are going to make a web page of your team and do it with google before asking for help" WTF !!!!! I have no idea about anything !!!! They were many hours in front of the computer, since in addition, the classes are not face-to-face due to the covid. I met new people, I have 2 great teachers and we have a different study method than the conventional one. But it was very funny. I really hope I can become a great programmer
